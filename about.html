About me

I am a Brazilian Junior Front-End Developer and an experienced Geological Engineer with Master's degree. Hands-on and passionate about generating ideas, since 2020, I have been learning a diversity of languages and technologies through a practical basis. Regarding my professional experience, as a geologist, I worked for six years as a consultant, building geological for technical reports, as a university professor, teaching technical design for civil engineering, and as a content creator, supporting educational institutions by creating didactic materials in a wide range of formats.

Looking for a career and a life change, in 2020, I left Brazil and moved to Portugal. A year later, while working as a customer support, I started to use my free time to learn more about coding and joined SheCodes Pro, an online course designed to become a professional developer.

Through coding I genuinely find great fulfillment in finding the best ways for the projects I embrace. Also, I am eager to learn, attentive to details, own excellent communication skills and punctual in my deliveries. Some things that inspire me are mountain landscapes, good music, minerals, Buddhism, and collage art.
